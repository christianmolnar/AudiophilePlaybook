Astell & Kern Kann Max

Headphone Playbook
I created this page to share my favorite EQ tuning ideas for Warm, Max Bass, and Relaxed curve signatures on the Kann Max, paired with my headphones and IEMs. This project was made in partnership with Orionis Cantus (OpenAI, ChatGPT)—a very helpful (and sometimes a bit misleading!) AI agent. I hope these tunings inspire your own listening adventures!

The Astell&Kern Kann Max is A&K's flagship digital audio player (DAP) renowned for its powerful amplification, pristine sound quality, and versatile output options. Designed for audiophiles who demand uncompromising performance, the Kann Max delivers exceptional clarity, dynamic range, and detail, making it ideal for driving even the most demanding headphones. Its compact form factor, robust build, and advanced DAC/amp architecture ensure a premium listening experience whether at home or on the go.

Audeze LCD-2 Classic

A legendary planar magnetic headphone, the LCD-2 Classic is beloved for its rich, smooth sound and deep, controlled bass. It’s a favorite for relaxed, immersive listening and excels with genres from jazz to electronic. The open-back design delivers a wide, natural soundstage.
  • Driver: Planar Magnetic
  • Impedance: 70Ω
  • Sensitivity: 101dB/1mW
  • Weight: 550g
Matching IEMs: KZ ZAS, TangZu Shimin Li

Focal Celestee

The Celestee is a closed-back dynamic headphone with a punchy, lively sound and luxurious build. Its tight bass and sparkling highs make it great for modern music, and its isolation is perfect for on-the-go or office use.
  • Driver: 40mm Aluminum/Magnesium Dynamic
  • Impedance: 35Ω
  • Sensitivity: 105dB/1mW
  • Weight: 430g
Matching IEMs: KZ ZS10 Pro, Moondrop Variations

Sennheiser HD800

Renowned for its immense soundstage and detail, the HD800 is a reference open-back headphone. It’s ideal for classical, acoustic, and critical listening, revealing every nuance in your music.
  • Driver: 56mm Dynamic
  • Impedance: 300Ω
  • Sensitivity: 102dB/1V
  • Weight: 330g
Matching IEMs: Moondrop Stellaris

Sennheiser HD599

A fantastic entry-level open-back, the HD599 offers a warm, comfortable sound signature and lightweight fit. It’s great for long listening sessions and pairs well with a wide range of music.
  • Driver: Dynamic
  • Impedance: 50Ω
  • Sensitivity: 106dB/1V
  • Weight: 250g

Hifiman Audivina

A closed-back planar magnetic headphone with a unique resonance chamber, the Audivina delivers fast, detailed sound and strong bass. It’s excellent for both home and portable use, with great isolation.
  • Driver: Planar Magnetic
  • Impedance: 20Ω
  • Sensitivity: 97dB/1mW
  • Weight: 470g
Matching IEMs: KZ ZSN Pro X, Letshuoer DZ4

Hifiman Ananda Nano

The Ananda Nano features an ultra-thin diaphragm for lightning-fast response and airy, open sound. It’s a superb all-rounder for those who want detail, comfort, and a wide soundstage.
  • Driver: Planar Magnetic
  • Impedance: 14Ω
  • Sensitivity: 94dB/1mW
